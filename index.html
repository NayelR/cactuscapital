cactus capital

we're high schoolers from northern virginia looking to fund cool projects

we provide student builders with microgrants of $50-100 to help them get started

you can be at any stage with your project, it can even be just an idea you have

we don't want equity; but if you make it big, all we ask is that you let us borrow your jet

interested? apply here. if your idea is compelling, we'll reach out for an chat

looking to help fund young builders? email us at contact@cactuscapital.org
